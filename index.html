About The Grassroots

Results-based monitoring for development that takes root on the ground
THE GRASSROOTS

The Grassroots shows whether development work is actually making a difference. Across a portfolio of country programmes it brings the whole picture into one view: an interactive world map, faceted filters, a live activity feed and an insights dashboard, all aligned to the Sustainable Development Goals.

Everything hangs on one results chain: Plan → Impact → Outcome → Output → Activity. Each multi-year Plan holds its own Impacts; Impacts break down into the Outcomes that drive them, Outcomes into the Outputs that deliver them, and each Output is measured by one or more KPIs. Activities are the individual updates logged against those KPIs in the field, so a single report in one country rolls all the way up to the Impact and Plan it belongs to. Switch the active plan from the Results Framework panel.

Status is never chosen by hand. For every KPI the platform reads the baseline, the target and the latest measurement, then derives Progress (how far the result has come toward its target) and Performance (whether that progress keeps pace with the time elapsed). Together they produce the Red / Amber / Green rating on every map bubble, KPI card and roll-up.

Two chains meet at every KPI: a results chain from Impact down to Activity, and a delivery chain from Donor to Project to Activity, so results and spending read side by side. Field teams simply log activities; every status, total and trend above them is derived, never retyped.
